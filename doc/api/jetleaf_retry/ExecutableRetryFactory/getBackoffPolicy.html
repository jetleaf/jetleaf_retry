BackoffPolicy getBackoffPolicy()

Returns the BackoffPolicy governing the computation of delay durations between consecutive retry attempts.

The backoff policy controls the timing of retries, helping to mitigate overload or network congestion by spacing out repeated invocations.

Typical backoff strategies include:

  • Constant delay (fixed interval)
  • Exponential backoff with jitter
  • Custom adaptive delays

Example

final delay = factory.getBackoffPolicy().computeBackoff(context);
await Future.delayed(delay);

See also

  • RetryPolicy for controlling whether retries are allowed.
  • RetryExecutor for coordinating retries and delays.
